How we work

A clear, structured process that reduces stress and keeps you informed – from first ideas to final styling.

  1. 01. Site analysis, design & material consultation

    We visit your property in Manchester or the surrounding area to analyse the site conditions, discuss your ideas and budget, and help you choose layouts, tiles, colours and finishes.

  2. 02. Detailed quotation

    You receive a clear, itemised quotation with defined scope, materials and timelines, so you know exactly what is included.

  3. 03. Planning & scheduling

    We coordinate trades, deliveries and access to minimise disruption and keep the project running smoothly.

  4. 04. Installation & finishes

    Experienced tilers, fitters and joiners carry out the works with a strong focus on detail, cleanliness and safety. Regular checks ensure consistent quality.

  5. 05. Styling & handover

    We walk you through the finished space, deal with any snags and remain available for follow-up support.

Frequently asked questions

A few common questions our clients ask before starting a high-standard bathroom, kitchen or interior project.

We are a new company – how much experience do you really have?

While the company name is new, our team has many years of experience on UK residential projects, including high-end bathrooms, kitchens, joinery and extensions. We decided to set up a smaller, more focused company to work directly with clients who care about quality.

How do you price projects?

We provide a detailed, itemised quotation based on the agreed scope, materials and access. For most projects we work on a fixed-price basis, so you know the investment up front. We are rarely the cheapest quote, but we aim to be the most reliable.

What budgets do you typically work with?

As a rough guide, most bathroom projects start from around £5,000, kitchens from £10,000 and full interior projects from £25,000. Actual costs depend on size, specification and any structural work.

Can you help us with design and material choices?

Yes. We regularly help clients choose layouts, tiles, sanitaryware, kitchen fronts, worktops and joinery details. We do not replace a full interior designer, but we can guide you to a clean, modern look that fits your property and budget.

How far in advance should we book?

Because we only take on a limited number of projects at a time, our schedule can book up several weeks or months ahead. If you have a fixed deadline, it is best to contact us as early as possible.
