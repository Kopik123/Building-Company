# Level + Lines - Designer Brief

## Brand Position
- Brand: `Level + Lines`
- Market: affluent homeowners in Manchester and Greater Manchester.
- Offer: premium bathrooms, kitchens, interiors, and selective full-home upgrades.
- Tone: calm confidence, precision, symmetry, understated luxury.

## Visual Direction
- Core principle: clean straight lines, balance, geometric clarity.
- Style: premium contemporary with restrained contrast and intentional whitespace.
- Logo: use only `logo4.png` across all pages.
- No monogram fallback (`BC`) in production layout.

## Color System
- Primary dark: deep charcoal/navy for authority and depth.
- Accent: warm metal gold for premium cues.
- Light surfaces: warm neutrals for readability and elegance.
- Contrast target: maintain comfortable accessibility for body and CTA text.

## Typography
- Display: `Cormorant Garamond` for premium editorial feel.
- Body/UI: `Montserrat` for clean readability.
- Keep headline rhythm spacious and deliberate.

## Header Direction (Ultra-Premium)
- Minimal header with logo-first composition.
- Centered navigation.
- Reduced visual noise, no decorative badge mark.
- CTA wording: `Request private consultation`.

## UX and Motion
- Subtle reveal effects and transitions.
- Motion should support hierarchy, not distract.
- Strong mobile behavior and simple, predictable interactions.

## Conversion Positioning
- Lead qualification language remains premium and direct.
- Contact form captures project type, location, and budget range.
- Copy should signal selective intake and quality-first delivery.

## Content Structure
- Home page: premium positioning, services, gallery, process, trust, FAQ, contact.
- Service pages: bathrooms and kitchens with same premium visual language.
- Legal pages: aligned brand naming and consistent typography.

## Final Lock Notes
- Production design baseline is locked under `design-lock-v1`.
- Future edits should be limited to critical fixes or approved content updates.
